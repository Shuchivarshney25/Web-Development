MS Dhoni

Description

Mahendra Singh Dhoni or M S Dhoni is one of the most popular Indian cricketers.

Putting his great leadership skills to test, he served as the former captain of the Indian national cricket team; however, he captained the team only in limited-overs format.

Also known for his excellent wicket-keeping skills, he had always been a gem to the Indian team even in some very hard situations.

He made his debut in One Day International (ODI) in December 2004, playing against Bangladesh. A year later, he debuted in test cricket against Sri Lanka.
